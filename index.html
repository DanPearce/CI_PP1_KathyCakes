About Us

Here at KathyCakes we strive to provide our customers with the best cakes and recipes you can find in store and online! That's why all our baking goods are home-made and use only the best sources for ingredients. Organic and home-made is in our core so you’re guaranteed a delicious and succulent dessert to make you want to come back for more!

We’re often asked where we started and how we got our name! KathyCakes was founded in Bristol, UK in the early 2000s and is inspired from our Head Chef and CEO Kath! She used to bring cakes into her old job to share with her colleagues when she realised, she had the potential to start her own business as they tasted so good! All her colleagues used to love it when she’d bring some tasty treats to share! The positive feedback she received gave her the courage to break free and start her own bakery!

Now running for over 10 years KathyCakes is a staple of Bristolian life and sets the bar high when in the search for baked goodness! So why not stop on by, come on in and grab yourself a treat!

Our Signature Recipes

Apple & Cinnamon Crumble Cupcakes

Delicious Apple Cupcakes topped with a succulent cinnamon buttercream with an added dollop of salted caramel. These are perfect for the festive season and one of our bakery’s favourites to serve.

Raspberry Twist Cupcakes

Organic and natural Raspberry Twist Cupcakes, topped with a fruity raspberry frosting and freshly picked raspberries. These cupcakes are spectacular and draw your eyes right to them, vivid in colour and in flavour.

Grandma's Signature Lemon Loaf

Soft and tender to the touch, Grandma’s Signature Lemon Loaf is the zingiest loaf cake you’ll ever try and will make you want more and more. Inspired from our Head Chef’s Grandma this home-made recipe is made to be tasted.
